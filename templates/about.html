About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Vlad and I'm really excited about this blog. I like videogames, reading and music. But most of all I like making things, like this blog. Recently I started learning programming and with the knowledge I gained I started this little project. I'll be posting pretty random stuff. But I thought I'd introduce myself just so you know a little about me and what you'll be seeing here.

I live in London where I work as a bartender while I study and try to break in the tech industry. I spend most of my days either working, studying or job hunting. But on my free time I do so many things I had to start scheduling my hobbies. I love sitting around with my guitar and just play until my fingers hurt. I love sports and I recently got into bouldering (and I'm pretty good at it). I love videogames and I could spend hours playing and talking about them. Some of my favourites are "The last of us" and "Horizon" (the one about the robots, not the racing one). I love travelling and I do it quite often. I'm also really into reading, especially fantasy novels. I'm a big fan of the Brandon Sanderson's "Mistborn" saga, Patrick Rothfuss' "King killer chronicles" and Sarah J. Maas' "Throne of glass" saga. I'm a major cinephile but there are a lot of classics I still haven't seen. I didn't really have a lot of free time since I moved to London.

That's pretty much it. Me in a nutshell. I hope you have a good time reading my ramblings.
